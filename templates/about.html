About the System

The Diabetes Risk Prediction System is an AI-powered tool that helps identify individuals at risk of developing diabetes and provides personalized health recommendations.

How It Works

This system uses machine learning algorithms trained on clinical data to assess an individual's risk of developing diabetes based on various health parameters. The prediction model analyzes these inputs and generates a risk score and categorization.

Features

  • Accurate risk prediction based on personal health data
  • Risk categorization into Low, Medium, and High risk groups
  • Personalized recommendations tailored to individual health profiles
  • Easy-to-understand risk visualization
  • Printable reports for sharing with healthcare providers

Data Privacy

The system processes all data locally in your browser. No personal health information is stored on our servers. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security.

Medical Disclaimer

This tool is designed for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

Input Parameters

The system uses the following health parameters:

  • Pregnancies: Number of times pregnant
  • Glucose: Plasma glucose concentration
  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
  • Insulin: 2-Hour serum insulin (μU/ml)
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (kg/m²)
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A function that scores likelihood of diabetes based on family history
  • Age: Age in years
